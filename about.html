About Us

Afghanistan Humanitarian National Organization (AHNO)

AHNO is Non – Governmental, non – Profit, non – Political and Non – Sectarian Organization working in Afghanistan. Registered with the MOEc, reg. no. (5763). AHNO Have well qualified, experienced and ambitious staff and members, aiming to server the Afghan People in various humanitarian fields.

Vision

To have heathy, educated, and economically independent societies in Afghanistan, to step out from emergency situation to a prosperous nation. And to live with dignity and honor.

Mission

To mobilize Humanitarian assistance and reach out to the people in need. Deliver basic and lifesaving assistance swiftly and cost effectively to the Afghan people wherever they located in Afghanistan.

Goal

To mobilize humanitarian aid, ease its delivery, and manage its distribution to maximize its impact in serving the vulnerable Afghan population across Afghanistan.

Value

➦ Respecting local religious and cultural norms.
➦ To work with empathy, tolerance, loyalty, integrity and peace, keeping in view gender sensitivity, women & child rights and environment.
➦ Honesty, commitment, transparency and courageous that makes us accountable for all our stakeholders at any phase of project implementation.
➦ To work independently with communities irrespective of their creed, race, gender, faction, ethnic, sect or political affiliation.
➦ Learning from experiences and adding them to our resources to share with all stakeholder.
